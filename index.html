    About Us

    Helping Hand International is a Non-Profit Organization from Gujarat, India. Our organization was established in the year of 2016 under the leadership of Dhruv Panchal.We believe that empowering others can empower ourselves. Being a part of the Helping Hand International is a wonderful experience of bringing the change by empowering others. In this article, we would be highlighting the achievements of our organization in bringing a positive change in our country India.

    Our Mission

    We believe in the empowerment of the people by way of awareness, capacity building and skill development, as the charity alone cannot bring the required development. Hence, Weaker People in general, Children & women in particular.

    Our Objectives

    Develop a global platform of youth activists and organizations across the globe.
    Achieve the United Nations’ vision 2030 by following 17 Sustainable Development Goals.

    Motivate young social activists to become active agents of socio-economic change.

    Our Vision

    Connect youth across the world for making a better world, We assist the Weaker People in improving their Standard of living by focusing on “Income generation, Health, Education, Clean Environment and Social Justice, Peace, UN SDG17, Spreading Peace, Social activism in youth around the world”
